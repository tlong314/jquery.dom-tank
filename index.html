jQuery.htmlTank Example

Click on a DOM element - the title above, the paragraph below, the square below it, and it will transform into an htmlTank.
Once transformed, you can move it around the page and randomly shoot around. A black rectangle will show up on the bottom right of the screen which you can use for your mouse controls. Put the cursor over it (the pointer will disappear),

and LEFT CLICK to move the left wheels forward, RIGHT CLICK to move the right wheels forward, CENTER CLICK to move both and move your tank forward.

You can also use the arrow keys, or ASDW (old school) to move your htmlTank.

Press ENTER to fire.

Press ESC to remove the tank and start over.
